Landscaping Company SEO: Capturing Seasonal Local Demand

Landscaping is one of the most seasonal local service categories. Demand swings 5x between peak and off-season. Marketing that ignores seasonality wastes money in slow months and leaves money on the table in peak months.

Smart landscaping marketing front-loads investment in February-March for the spring rush, focuses on retention through summer, and shifts to fall cleanup and snow services in autumn.

Landscaping SEO essentials

  • Seasonal landing pages updated monthly (spring planting, summer maintenance, fall cleanup)
  • GBP posts published weekly during peak season
  • Photo and video uploads of recent projects
  • Reviews from real customers specific to each service type
  • Service-area definition matched to realistic coverage
  • Content for both residential and commercial audiences if both are served

Why recurring service contracts change the math

A one-time mowing customer is worth $100-$200. A seasonal contract customer is worth $1500-$5000 per year. Marketing should prioritize the channels and content that bring in contract customers, not one-off jobs.
